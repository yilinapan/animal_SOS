# Phase 1 — Discovery: 60+ Platforms Worldwide

Research conducted June 2026 via systematic web research across four regional/thematic sweeps. **Method caveat:** several target sites block automated fetching; homepage-section details and self-reported statistics are drawn from search-indexed content, official sub-pages, press, and secondary sources, and organizational reach claims are not independently audited. Items the researchers could not verify are flagged in the detailed regional notes.

The platforms cluster into five archetypes, which matters for fair comparison (see [Phase 2](02-scoring-framework.md)):
- **E** — Emergency dispatch / triage (get this animal help now)
- **D** — Directory / signposting (who near me can help)
- **C** — Citizen science / observation (log what I saw)
- **G** — Government / welfare reporting (cruelty, strays, legal duty)
- **H** — Hospital / rescue org (care endpoint) · **L** — Lost & found pets · **A** — Adoption · **X** — Advocacy/education · **T** — Tech/UX pattern source
